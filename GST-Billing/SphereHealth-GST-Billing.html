📖 GST Consultant Notes — Rate & SAC Rationale

Supplier: SPHEREHEALTH MEDICAL SOLUTIONS PVT. LTD., GSTIN 06ABNCS8193D1ZX, Haryana (State 06), Regular taxpayer.

1. Medical equipment on rent (bed, suction, BiPAP, nebulizer, monitor 3/5-para, O₂ concentrator, O₂ cylinder) → SAC 997319, GST 12%. Leasing/renting of goods is taxed at the same rate as supply of the like goods (Notfn 11/2017-CT(R), Heading 9973). Medical devices fall under Chapter 90 / medical oxygen, taxed at 12%.

2. GDA / Attendant at home → SAC 998514, GST 18%. Supply of manpower / temporary staffing. A non-clinical attendant is not “healthcare”, so it is taxable at 18%.

3. Nursing care at home (registered nurse) → SAC 999316, Exempt. Health-care service by para-medics (Notfn 12/2017-CT(R), Sl. 74, Heading 9993).

4. Doctor visit at home → SAC 999312, Exempt. Health-care service by an authorised medical practitioner.

5. ECG at home (diagnostic + report) → SAC 999316, Exempt. Diagnostic health-care service. (If you only rent out the ECG machine without a report, treat it as 12% equipment rental instead.)

6. SphereHealth software — one-time license → SAC 997331, GST 18%. Sale of a one-time license of your offline utility tool to other hospitals = licensing the right to use computer software (taxable at 18%, whether delivered online or on media). Price is fully flexible — quote any amount (₹1,000 / ₹7,000 / ₹15,000 …) per customer. Two catalog picks are provided: “price INCLUDES GST” (tool back-calculates base + 18%, e.g. ₹10,000 → ₹8,474.58 + ₹1,525.42) and “GST EXTRA @18%” (your amount is the base, GST added on top, e.g. ₹10,000 → ₹10,000 + ₹1,800 = ₹11,800). Switch any line’s mode anytime with the “Incl?” column.

Document type (auto): all-taxable → Tax Invoice; all-exempt → Bill of Supply; mixed → Invoice-cum-Bill of Supply (Rule 46 / 46A).
CGST+SGST vs IGST (auto): customer in Haryana → CGST 50% + SGST 50%; any other state → IGST full.

Disclaimer: These are standard, defensible classifications. Whether your firm qualifies as a “clinical establishment” for the healthcare exemption, and the exact rate on a specific device, should be confirmed with your Chartered Accountant before filing. Every line item rate is editable.
